Interaction Flow: Creating a Dog Meme PostModel in the Talking PetModel App

Mary wants to create and share a funny meme of her poodle on social media.

Precondition

Mary has an account on the Talking PetModel app, has created memes before, and is logged in.

Postcondition

Mary can easily find her meme in the app and share it on her social media platforms.

Interaction Flow

  • Mary opens the Talking PetModel app on her phone and scans the home screen for the “Create Meme” button.

  • The app takes her to the meme creation page and displays an option to record her dog’s bark.

  • Mary taps the “Record” button and captures her poodle’s bark.

  • The app processes the recording and generates a humorous caption based on the bark.

  • Mary reviews the generated caption and taps the “Edit” button to make slight adjustments.

  • After editing, Mary selects the “Add Image” button to upload a cute picture of her poodle.

  • The app displays the image upload interface, allowing Mary to choose a recent photo from her gallery.

  • Mary selects her favorite picture of her poodle and clicks “Use This Photo.”

  • The app combines the edited caption with the chosen image, displaying a preview of the meme.

  • Mary reviews the meme and taps the “Share” button.

  • The app prompts her to select social media platforms to share the meme.

  • Mary chooses The PetModel talking, Instagram and Facebook and clicks “PostModel.”

  • The app confirms that her meme has been successfully shared on her selected platforms, and Mary receives notifications of interactions from friends.
